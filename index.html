Rating Some Books I've Recently Read:

The Way I Used To Be follows the journey of a young woman named Eden
after being raped by her brother's best friend. It is told in four parts --
her freshman, sophomore, junior, and senior year of high school --
as she struggles to find strength.

Archer's Voice is about a woman name Bree Prescott, who is attached to
the memory of a horrifying night and a man named Archer Hale,
who lives with a wound from a traumatic experience. Archer's love is the key
to her freedom and Bree is the key to helping him find his voice.

The Cheat Sheet is a sweet rom-com about two best friends, Bree Camden
and Nathan Donelson, who are secretly in love with each other. When Bree
accidentally gets drunk and tells a reporter she is with her famous quarterback
best friend, they have to fake date publicly for three weeks.

Until Friday Night is about a girl named Maggie Carleton, who goes mute
after telling police about how she witnessed her father murdering her
mother. Maggie moves to Lawton to live with her cousin, aunt, and uncle,
where she meets West. West is a popular jock, who battles with grief watching
his father slowly dying. When West finds the pain too much to handle,
he talks to the girl in the shadows (Maggie) at a party, but he never expected a reply.

Josh and Hazel's Guide to Not Dating is about a woman named Hazel Camille Bratford,
who is too much for most men to handle and a man named Josh Im, who just found out
his girlfriend cheated on him. Hazel convinces Josh to set each other up on blind dates, but when
the blind dates seem to not be working, their attraction for each other seems to grow.

The Unhoneymooners is about a woman named Olive, who is very unlucky. She has a twin sister
named Ami, who is the luckiest person in the world and getting married. When the entire wedding
party gets food poisoning, Olive and the best man, Ethan has to go on the all-expense-paid honeymoon in
Hawaii. At Hawaii, Olive bumps into her future boss, so her and Ethan have to pretend to be loving newlyweds.

Dead Poets Society is about an English teacher, who inspires students by his teaching of poetry.
The teacher says "Seize the day, boys. Make your life extraordinary.", which inspires them to
re-establish a secret club called "Dead Poets Society". This club is where they are free from
everything and let their passions run free.

Orbiting Jupiter is a heartbreaking book about two boys, Joseph and Jack. Joseph is Jack's new
foster brother, who became a father at thirteen, but has never got to see his daughter. It follows the journey
of the two boys, who find out the meaning of family and the required sacrifices needed for it.

We Were Liars is about the Sinclair family, who seems to be perfect, but they
hide their secrets, lies, and flaws behind their tall and beautiful build.

Little Fires Everywhere is about two famillies, the Richardson's and the Warren's. Mia and Peral Warren move to Shaker Heights
renting a house from the Richardson's. The Richardson children become more curious about the mother-daughter duo and the
Warren's become more than just tenants, making Elena Richardson more interested in revealing Mia's secrets. Family friends of
the Richardsons try to adopt a Chinese-American baby, but as the custody battle begins, it divides the town, putting Elena Richardson
and Mia Warren on different sides.

The perks of being a wallflower is about the first year of high school for a boy named Charlie.
It shows Charlie's struggles with trying to fit in during his first year with new friends, suicide, drugs, and abuse.

Lord of the Flies is about a group of young boys who become stranded on a deserted island as a result of a plane crash.
They have to figure out how to survive without any adults, making their own rules and system.
